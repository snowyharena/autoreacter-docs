# Whitelist

Whitelist restricts auto-reactions to only trigger for specific roles, members, or words.

## Usage

When you add items to a whitelist, the bot **only reacts** to messages matching the whitelist filters. Messages that don't match are completely ignored.

## Limits

| Item | Free | Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Whitelist roles | 2 | 30 |
| Whitelist members | 5 | 50 |
| Whitelist words | 2 | 30 |
| Word character limit | 30 | 200 |

## Commands

- `/whitelist role` - Whitelist roles
- `/whitelist member` - Whitelist members  
- `/whitelist word` - Whitelist keywords
