# /premium activate

Activate premium features in your server.

## Usage

`/premium activate`

## What It Does

- Enables premium for your server
- Unlocks 500 auto-reaction slots (vs 10 free)
- Increases all whitelist/blacklist/manager limits
- Faster reaction speeds
- Requires active premium subscription
