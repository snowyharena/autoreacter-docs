# What is AutoReacter?

**AutoReacter** is a Discord bot that automatically applies emoji reactions to messages based on your customizable rules. Save time, increase engagement, and automate your server's reactions!

## Key Features

<Icon icon="filter" iconType="solid" /> **Message Type Filters** - React to All Messages, Text Only, Images, Videos, Media, or Links

<Icon icon="sliders" iconType="solid" /> **Advanced Modifications** - Ignore replies, bots, webhooks, members, and more

<Icon icon="shield" iconType="solid" /> **Whitelist & Blacklist** - Control who can trigger reactions with roles, members, or words

<Icon icon="users" iconType="solid" /> **Manager System** - Let members configure AutoReacter without needing administrator permissions
