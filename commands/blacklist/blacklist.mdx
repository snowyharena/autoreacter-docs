# Blacklist

Blacklist prevents auto-reactions from triggering for specific roles, members, or words.

## Usage

When you add items to a blacklist, the bot **ignores** messages matching the blacklist filters. Everything else gets reactions normally.

## Limits

| Item | Free | Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Blacklist roles | 2 | 30 |
| Blacklist members | 5 | 50 |
| Blacklist words | 2 | 30 |
| Word character limit | 30 | 200 |

## Commands

- `/blacklist role` - Blacklist roles
- `/blacklist member` - Blacklist members
- `/blacklist word` - Blacklist keywords
