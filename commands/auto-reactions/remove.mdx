# /autoreaction remove

Remove one or more configured auto-reactions from your server.

## Permissions

- **Requires:** Administrator or Manager

## Usage

`/autoreaction remove`

1. Select the auto-reactions you want to remove from the dropdown and confirm

## Bulk Removal

If you have multiple auto-reactions, use the **Remove All Auto-Reactions** button to delete all auto-reactions in your server at once.

## Pagination

If you have more than 20 auto-reactions:
- Results show 20 per page
- Use the back and forward buttons to navigate pages
- Switching pages resets your current selection
